[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire when our shadows intertwine
A slow and steady heat is running down my spine
Your fingertips are tracing maps upon my skin
This is the only place I ever want to begin

[Verse 1]
The porch light flickers but we do not need the sun
Our shadows tangle on the wall and move as one
I taste the honey and the summer on your lips
My hands are sliding down the curve around your hips

[Pre-Chorus]
The air is thick with every secret that we keep
We are swimming into waters beautiful and deep
You pull my collar and you pull me to the floor
I know exactly what you are quietly asking for

[Chorus]
Oh, I am swaying to your gentle rhythm
Caught inside the heat we make tonight
Our bodies moving, nothing is hidden
Burning up the dark until the light

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, your gentle rhythm, pulling me in
Your gentle rhythm, over my skin

[Verse 2]
The wooden stairs are creaking just beneath our weight
We leave our heavy clothes beside the iron grate
Your skin is glowing like a lantern in the gloom
We bring the thunderstorm into this quiet room

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The air is thick with every secret that we keep
We are swimming into waters beautiful and deep
You pull my collar and you pull me to the bed
I listen closely to the words you never said

[Chorus]
Oh, I am swaying to your gentle rhythm
Caught inside the heat we make tonight
Our bodies moving, nothing is hidden
Burning up the dark until the light

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, your gentle rhythm, pulling me in
Your gentle rhythm, over my skin

[Bridge]
Your gentle rhythm is the only thing I need
I am following wherever you will lead
I trace the poetry you write upon my chest
You know exactly how to put me to the test
When you whisper Saeed softly in the dark
You ignite my soul and leave a burning mark

[Spoken]
There is nowhere else I would ever rather be
Just let this midnight swallow you and me

[Final Chorus]
Oh, I am swaying to your gentle rhythm
Caught inside the heat we make tonight
Our bodies moving, nothing is hidden
Burning up the dark until the light
